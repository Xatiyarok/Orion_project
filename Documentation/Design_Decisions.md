# Design Decisions

## Why MAX-M10S?

- Modern GNSS receiver
- Low power consumption
- Excellent sensitivity
- Well documented

## Why ESP32-S3?

- Powerful MCU
- Future AI capabilities
- WiFi/Bluetooth
- USB support

## Why UART?

Simple interface.

Well supported.

Low hardware complexity.

## Why U.FL?

Allows experimentation with different GNSS antennas.

Easy replacement.

Professional RF connector.

## Why Two Layers?

Educational objective.

Lower manufacturing cost.

Simpler assembly.

Future revisions will evaluate a four-layer PCB.
